Employment Opportunities At ArtisTech - January 2007

ArtisTech, Inc. is currently seeking several strong Software Engineers for immediate full-time positions.  Here are the job descriptions as they appear in the Washington Post Online Job Advertisement.

To apply or inquire please send an email with an electronic copy of your resume to, jobs@artistech.com

SOFTWARE ENGINEER - Research

US Citizenship is required

ArtisTech, Inc. is seeking a candidate to join our research team. This position will require working closely with our lead developer on expanding support for an Army research project.

Successful candidate will design and implement solutions that apply cutting edge intelligent systems and cognitive science research to military solutions for experiments with the Army Research Laboratory.

These technologies include machine reasoning, cognitive models, simulations, advanced visualizations, intelligent planning systems, and next generation human interface devices.

Responsibilities may also include assisting with experiments, demonstrating research to Army stakeholders, and authoring technical papers. Candidate will also investigate applications for intelligent systems and sensor fusion in the field of autonomous surveillance and tracking.

B.S. in Computer Science, M.S. preferred.
3-5 years experience.
Extensive experience with C#, Java, or C++ preferred.
Extensive experience with algorithms, AI, or graphics preferred.
Experience with SOA’s, COM, or distributed systems a plus.
Proven written and verbal communication.
Self-starter, innovative, creative, and flexible.

Must be able obtain a security clearance. Current DoD Clearance is a plus.
 
SOFTWARE ENGINEER - Multi-Lingual Computing

US Citizenship is required

ArtisTech, Inc. is searching for strong Software Engineers to join a multi-disciplinary team of experts developing emerging technology to advance multi-lingual computing, to work in our MD and VA locations.

This is a fast-paced research and development program where the successful candidate will need to learn new application and development domain technologies independently on-the-job.

Development languages include:
.NET, Java, and scripting for implementing cross-platform, distributed applications and web-services.


M.S. in Computer Science (or related field)
3-5 years experience
Networking experience is helpful
Experience with Linux and Solaris a plus.
Multi-Lingual is a plus

Candidates must have strong verbal and written communication skills and the desire to work closely with the customer.

Must be able obtain a security clearance.

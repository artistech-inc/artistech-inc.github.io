Software Engineer, Listing HLTSD1203

ArtisTech, Inc., a rapidly growing technology services firm, is seeking software engineers who are eager to work on challenging projects in a fast-paced but casual environment. Areas of focus include multimedia streaming, 3D visualizations, artificial intelligence, distributed computing, and GIS applications.

Candidates should have a solid background in software development, be able to learn new technologies and systems quickly, and be excited to work in a team environment building next-generation solutions for the government and commercial sector.

Candidates should also be proficient in one of the following: C++, C#, or Java. .NET, J2EE, or Pocket PC/Linux PDA experience is a plus. XML or SQL knowledge is also a benefit.
U.S. Citizenship is required. A, B.S. in C.S. or equivalent is required; a Master's degree in C.S. or equivalent is a plus.

TSSCI Clearance is required.

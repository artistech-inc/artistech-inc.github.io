Welcome

ArtisTech, Inc. is engaged in the critical process of transforming leading research into applications for the U.S. Department of Defense and the Intelligence Community.  By combining pivotal technologies with the right people and the right processes, ArtisTech provides systems to meet the mission, enterprise and infrastructure needs of our customers.    

ArtisTech

  • Uses strong software engineering practices to apply advanced technology to real problems
  • Integrates breakthrough technologies such as ontologies and agent architectures in customer relevant ways
  • Works well in integrated U.S. Government/Contractor teams to design, build, test, and deliver working solutions to war-fighters
  • Makes complex technology intuitive and effective

Our engagements range from short analyses to multi-year R&D projects. We work on world-class research programs hand in hand with university and government researchers to bring new science to bear in systems ready for Tech Transfer.

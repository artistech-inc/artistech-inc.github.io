MT Outpost

In collaboration with ARL Researchers, we designed, implemented, tested, and continue to maintain an on-line component-based Outpost Machine Translation (MT) experiment system.  The Outpost MT system is a web-server based system.  ArtisTech and ARL have experience and access to many COTS and research MT systems; however, there are also research MT system subcomponents which are the result of Government, University, and Industry research efforts.  MT component improvement can change the utility of  MT to Army critical translation needs.

ArtisTech has conducted the software design and engineering to create a unique capability for the Multi-Lingual Computing Outpost, an open on-line MT system which integrates MT components from a variety of sources to facilitate ongoing research, and testing of emerging MT component technology advancements relative to Army mission requirements.
